🍅 Tomato Sorting System

AI Powered Tomato Quality Detection

This system uses Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to automatically detect and classify tomatoes into Good and Bad categories.

The system works using two powerful deep learning models:

YOLO Object Detection – Detects tomatoes in the image
EfficientNetB0 – Classifies tomato quality

Simply upload an image of tomatoes and the system will detect each tomato and determine whether it is healthy or defective.
